# Welcome

I am **Besufekad Tadele Hadero**, an M.S. Candidate in **Computer Science and Technology** at [Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU)](https://www.nwpu.edu.cn), advised by [Prof. Yang Tao](https://robotvisionlab.github.io) in the Robot Vision Lab. I will complete my degree in June 2026.

My research lies at the intersection of **machine learning**, **visual perception**, and **multimodal intelligence** for autonomous systems in complex real-world environments. I work on bridging classical geometric SLAM and modern neural scene representations to improve robustness and scalability.

## Research Interests

- **Visual SLAM & 3D Mapping**: Gaussian Splatting, NeRF-based representations, dense mapping
- **Monocular Depth Estimation**: Feed-forward geometry, metric-scale stability
- **Multimodal Perception**: Vision-Language-Action models, embodied intelligence
- **Autonomous Systems**: Real-time perception for robotics and autonomous driving
