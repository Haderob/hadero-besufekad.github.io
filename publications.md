## Survey & Review Contributions

My IEEE IoT Journal survey analyzes how 3D Gaussian Splatting surpasses NeRF-based methods in rendering efficiency and photometric consistency for SLAM, and positions feed-forward point-map pipelines (DUSt3R, MASt3R, VGGT) as a shift toward real-time mapping without costly iterative optimization.
